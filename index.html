What is Palladium?

Palladium is a Bitcoin fork that uses the same cryptographic algorithms for transactions, ensuring proven security and reliability. However, Palladium stands out with its average block time of just 2 minutes, allowing for significantly faster transaction validation compared to Bitcoin's 10 minutes.

The maximum supply is 21,000,000 PLM and a halving occurs every 210,000 blocks mined. Network security is maintained through the SHA256 proof-of-work algorithm, the same one used by Bitcoin to ensure the integrity of the blockchain.
