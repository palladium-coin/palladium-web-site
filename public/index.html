About

What is Palladium?

A Bitcoin fork built for speed, without sacrificing security.

Palladium inherits Bitcoin's cryptographic foundation — SHA-256 proof-of-work, a fixed supply, and a predictable halving schedule — while cutting block time from 10 minutes to 2. The result is a chain that is faster to confirm, easier to mine, and free from central control.
