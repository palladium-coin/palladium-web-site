Two Ways to Use Palladium

You can interact with the Palladium network in two different ways, depending on your needs and preferences:

PalladiumCore (Full Node)

The complete Palladium experience. PalladiumCore is our full node implementation that allows you to participate directly in the network consensus, validate transactions, and contribute to the blockchain's security and decentralization.

Ideal for: Miners, developers, and users who want full network participation and maximum security

  • Full blockchain validation and consensus participation
  • Complete transaction history and network state
  • Mining capabilities for network rewards
  • Maximum security and privacy
  • Support for advanced features and smart contracts
Download PalladiumCore

Electrum-based Light Wallet

A lightweight and user-friendly wallet solution based on the proven Electrum architecture. Perfect for everyday transactions without the need to download the entire blockchain or participate in network consensus.

Ideal for: Casual users who want simple balance management and quick transactions

  • Quick setup and instant synchronization
  • Minimal storage requirements
  • Fast transaction processing
  • User-friendly interface
  • Secure private key management
